Australia's Great Barrier Reef: One of the most impressive natural habitats in the world, the Great Barrier Reef could be killed by increased water temperatures and the resulting coral bleaching  . Australia is particularly easily damaged by global warming because of its large number of fragile   ecosystems, uncertain water sources and a large group of people gathering on the coast.
